robot 2.0

A science fiction film featuring Rajinikanth in a triple role as Vaseegaran, Chitti the Robot, and Kutti. Chitti is an advanced humanoid robot designed by Vaseegaran, with capabilities including a speed of 1 Terahertz and a memory capacity of 1 Zettabyte. In the film, Chitti 2.0 is an evil version of the robot, while Kutty is a microbot that can transform and disguise itself as a mobile phone.
